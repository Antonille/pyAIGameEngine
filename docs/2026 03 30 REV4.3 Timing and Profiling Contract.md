# 2026 03 30 REV4.3 Timing and Profiling Contract

## Purpose
Define the current timing surface that may be exposed to thin adapters and tests.

## Decided now
- Stage timing remains authoritative at the engine-loop stage level.
- The adapter-facing timing snapshot should expose:
  - `stage_summary`
  - `stage_avg_ms`
  - `last_input_application_summary`
  - `last_action_packet_summary`
  - `last_action_application_summary`
  - `last_ai_transfer_summary`
- Input-stage packet application is now surfaced explicitly for external-control adapters.

## Temporary scaffolding
- Timing is still lightweight and cumulative within the current process lifetime.
- The timing surface is sufficient for validation and adapter refit work, but not yet a full profiling product.

## Deferred
- Rich timeline traces.
- Cross-process / distributed timing aggregation.
- Full profiling export stack.

## Open questions
- Whether adapter-facing timing should later split packet-application cost by external vs scheduler-produced packets.
- How much timing detail should be archived per run without bloating the durable JSONL history.
